what we stand for

Building a better tommorow for Nigerians

The Ratal Movement AKA the 4th arm of the government is an organization that work tirelessly to help individuals seek justice, especially those who have been wronged or deprived of their rights. Through advocacy and direct intervention, we have assisted many in recovering their stolen goods and resolving disputes, ensuring their voices are heard and their needs are met.

Empowering communities to flourish in every aspect of life, we support youth development and growth through mentorship, education, and advocacy. Our mission is to inspire positive change, nurture potential, and help young people become leaders who shape a brighter future for all.
